Site Purpose

Name: TrothMoth. The reason why this name was chosen is because of its catchy label, along with its true meaning behind the name. The Google definition of the word "Troth" is "faith or loyalty when pledged in a solemn agreement or undertaking". I found that this word fits in with the theme of reliable moth facts, which is what this site provides.

Site Purpose: The site provides information about certain moth breeds along with riveting fun facts a majority of people may be unaware of. The site will hold information about different moth breeds and donating to support the company (along with donating to the cause of endangered moth species).

Scenarios

  1. What are the endangered moth species I should be aware of?
  2. What is the most common type of moth seen in North America?
  3. What role do moths play for the ecosystem?
